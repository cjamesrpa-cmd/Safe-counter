WHAT SAFECOUNTER BLOCKS — UNAUTHORIZED CHARGE BREAKDOWN

Personal Purchases
Employee buys personal items on company account. Category restrictions block non-work purchases before the transaction completes.

Expired Employee Access
Former or reassigned employee tries to use company account. Token generation fails immediately — no counter dispute needed.

Unauthorized Categories
Employee on a consumables-only job code tries to buy power tools. Category restrictions deny the purchase automatically.

Wrong Job Code Charges

Purchase at a distributor not approved for that job. Location-based restrictions flag or block off-list purchases entirely.
